Security

Security At InvoiceFlow, security is built into everything we do. We use industry-leading practices, modern infrastructure, and continuous monitoring to ensure your data is protected at all times. --- Data Protection Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using strong encryption standards. - TLS 1.2+ for all network communications - AES-256 encryption for stored data - Secure key management practices - Continuous vulnerability scanning We ensure that sensitive information is never exposed, logged insecurely, or shared without authorization. --- Authentication & Access Control We enforce strict access controls to ensure only authorized users can access data. - Secure login with strong password requirements - Role-based access control (RBAC) - Session management and automatic timeouts - Multi-factor authentication (MFA) support (coming soon) All access is logged and monitored for suspicious activity. --- Infrastructure Security Our platform runs on secure, globally trusted cloud infrastructure designed for high availability and resilience. - Firewalled and isolated environments - DDoS protection and traffic filtering - Regular system patching and updates - Redundant backups and disaster recovery We maintain uptime and protect against both internal and external threats. --- Monitoring & Threat Detection We continuously monitor our systems to detect and respond to threats in real time. - 24/7 system monitoring - Intrusion detection systems (IDS) - Automated alerts for unusual activity - Incident response procedures If a security issue arises, our team acts immediately to contain and resolve it. --- Testing & Vulnerability Management We proactively identify and fix security weaknesses. - Regular penetration testing - Automated vulnerability scans - Secure code reviews - Dependency and patch management Security is integrated into our development lifecycle. --- Compliance & Standards We follow globally recognized best practices and frameworks. - GDPR-aligned data handling - Secure data processing principles - Privacy-first architecture - Ongoing compliance improvements --- Backups & Recovery Your data is protected against loss with reliable backup systems. - Automated daily backups - Encrypted backup storage - Disaster recovery planning - Rapid restoration capabilities --- Responsible Disclosure We welcome security researchers and users to report vulnerabilities. If you discover a security issue, please contact us immediately: security@invoiceflow.com We will acknowledge and address all reports promptly. --- Your Responsibility Security is a shared responsibility. We recommend: - Using a strong, unique password - Keeping login credentials private - Logging out from shared devices - Reporting suspicious activity immediately --- Our Commitment We are committed to continuously improving our security practices to stay ahead of evolving threats. Your trust is our priority, and we take every measure to protect your data and privacy. ---
