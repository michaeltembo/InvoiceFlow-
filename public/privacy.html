🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

At InvoiceFlow, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our platform.

---

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide and improve our services. This may include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and contact details
  • Business Information: Company name, client details, and billing data
  • Invoice Data: Financial records, transactions, and payment status
  • Usage Data: Interaction with the platform, device, and browser information
---

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to operate and enhance the platform:

  • Provide and manage invoicing services

---

3. Data Storage & Retention

Your data is stored securely using modern storage practices:

  • Data may be stored locally (browser storage) or on secure servers
  • We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services
  • Backups may be maintained for disaster recovery purposes
---

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Regular security reviews and updates

While we take strong precautions, no system can be completely secure, and users share responsibility in protecting their credentials.

---

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party providers to support our services:

These providers are carefully selected and required to maintain appropriate security and confidentiality standards.

6. Your Rights & Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Access and review your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Requests can be made at any time through our support channels.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve your experience:
